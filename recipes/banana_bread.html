Banana Bread

By Elise Bauer

Description

A simple and delicious recipes for when you feel like putting those ripe bananas to use. Moist, delicious, and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 medium very ripe bananas peeled
  • 1/3 Cup (76g) butter, unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the pan
  2. Mash the bananas and add the butter
  3. Mix in the remaining ingredients

  4. Bake the bread

    Bake for 55 to 64 minutes at 350Â°F

  5. Cool and serve
